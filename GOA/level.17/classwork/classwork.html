The Best Gyms in Tbilisi, Georgia

Whether you are just passing through, or staying here long term as an expat, we’ve gathered all the information on the best athletics clubs in Tbilisi for you to choose from!

Prime Fit

Location: 71 Vazha-Pshavela Ave. (Saburtalo)

Prime Fit offers a swimming pool, jacuzzi, 2 saunas, spa with hammam, and a tanning solarium. They offer a wide variety of classes daily, including yoga, pilates, step dance, fitball, aerobics, abs, aqua, and even a kids dance class. They offer private trainers at additional cost for both the gym and the pool. Prime Fit has high reviews, and offers information on all of their classes in English on their Facebook page. Their membership fees are as follows:

  • 1-week – 50 GEL
  • 3-month – 550 GEL
  • 1-year – 1,490 GEL

Oktopus Fitness

Locations: 7 Sandro Euli Street (Saburtalo) 20 Nino Ramishvili Street (Vake) 1 Petre Kavtaradze Street (City Mall in Saburtalo) Oktopus Fitness is one of the most popular gym facilities in Georgia. They offer a wide variety of classes, including yoga, pilates, spinning, Zumba, HIIT, total ABS, and more. Their membership passes include 2 complimentary training sessions, a 1-day guest pass, and discounts on your 1st massage or tanning session. You can see the full list of inclusions, as well as any current promotions, on the English version of their website here. Their membership rates depend on which location you visit:
